Gooey Butter Bars
Author: Rose Bakes
January 15th, 2023

  • Prep Time 10 minutes
  • Cook Time 40 to 45 minutes
  • Total Time 55 minutes
  • Serving 24 bars
Ingredients
Crust
Filling
Instructions
  • 1 Preheat oven to 325°F and prepare a 9x13-inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray. Set aside.
  • 2 For the crust: Mix vanilla cake mix, butter, and egg in a large bowl with a fork until mixed together as thoroughly as possible. The texture of the mixture will be dry and clumpy.
  • 3 Transfer the mixture to the greased pan. Press the mixture down with your hands, covering the bottom of the pan as well as slightly up the sides of the pan.
  • 4 For the filling: Set aside about 1/4 cup of the powdered sugar. In a large bowl, mix the softened cream cheese, eggs, and vanilla. Slowly add in the remaining powdered sugar while mixing. When you're done mixing, the filling should be nice and creamy.
  • 5 To assemble: Pour the filling over the crust in the pan. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
  • 6 Remove the bars from the oven and let them cool. The filling will appear puffy, but will settle upon cooling. After about 10 minutes or so, sprinkle the remaining 1/4 cup powdered sugar over the top. Cool completely before serving.
