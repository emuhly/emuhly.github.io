Gooey Butter Bars
Author: Rose Bakes
January 15th, 2023

  • Prep Time 10 minutes
  • Cook Time 40 to 45 minutes
  • Total Time 55 minutes
  • Serving 12 meringues
Ingredients
Crust
Gooey Filling
Instructions
  • 1 Preheat oven to 325°F and prepare a 9x13-inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray. Set aside.
    Crust
  • 2 Mix vanilla cake mix, butter, and egg in a large bowl with a fork until mixed together as thoroughly as possible. The texture of the mixture will be dry and clumpy.
  • 3 Transfer the mixture to the greased pan. Press the mixture down with your hands, covering the bottom of the pan as well as slightly up the sides of the pan.
    Gooey Filling
  • 4 Set aside about 1/4 cup of the powdered sugar. When you're done mixing, the goo in the bowl should be nice and creamy.
  • 5 In a large bowl, mix the softened cream cheese, eggs, and vanilla. Slowly add in the remaining powdered sugar while mixing. When you're done mixing, the filling should be nice and creamy.
    Assembly
  • 6 Pour the filling over the crust in the pan. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
  • 7 Remove bars from the oven and let them cool. The filling will appear puffy, but will settle upon cooling. After about 10 minutes or so, sprinkle the remaining 1/4 cup powdered sugar over the top. Cool completely before serving.
