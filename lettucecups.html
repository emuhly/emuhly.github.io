Chicken Teriyaki Lettuce Cups
Author: John Beatty - Executive Chef at Catch NYC
January 20th, 2023

  • Prep Time 15 minutes
  • Cook Time 30 minutes
  • Total Time 45 minutes
  • Serving 4-6 large lettuce cups
Ingredients
Instructions
  • 1 Preheat oven to 450Â°. Toss sweet potatoes with 1 tbsp oil and 1/2 tsp salt. Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake until crispy, about 20 minutes. Let cool.
  • 2 Heat 1 tbsp oil in a medium nonstick skillet over medium high heat. Add chicken and cook, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned, about 3 minutes. Remove chicken from skillet.
  • 3 Heat remaining 1 tbsp oil in skillet over medium high; add zucchini and cook, stirring often, until tender, about 3 minutes. Return chicken to skillet; add teriyaki sauce, pepper, and remaining 1/4 tsp salt, and stir to combine.
  • 4 Stir together cornstarch and 1 tbsp cold water until smooth. Add to chicken mixture; cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 1 minute.
  • 5 Cut and remove core from bottom of lettuce and discard. Separate 4 to 6 large leaves and top with chicken mixture, then sprinkle with sweet potatoes.
